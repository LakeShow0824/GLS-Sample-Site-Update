The challenge
Why legacy servicers fall short

Platforms built for traditional mortgages can't handle the complexity of RTL, DSCR, and construction loans — and the workarounds create real costs for everyone.

Draw management
Slow, manual, error-prone
Draw requests, funding, and updates happen outside the system — creating reconciliation headaches, delays, and a borrower experience that reflects poorly on everyone in the chain.
Investor experience
Limited, outdated data
Batch files and end-of-day snapshots mean investors are always working with yesterday's picture of their portfolio. Real-time visibility is an afterthought, not a feature.
Operational risk
Off-platform activity
Key servicing activities happen outside the system — in spreadsheets and inboxes. Errors are inevitable and expensive to unwind.
