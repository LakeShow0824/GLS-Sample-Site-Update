Who we are
A different kind of servicer.

Why we built GLS
Mortgage servicing got left behind.

Most loan servicers run on technology that is decades old. Years of patches, workarounds, and bolt-on additions have kept them running — but they haven't kept pace with the leaps in technology that every other part of the financial industry has embraced. Banking modernized. Payments became instant. Accounting software gave professionals real-time visibility into everything. Mortgage servicing didn't.

We wouldn't ask a banking professional to run their work on decades-old systems. Borrowers, employees, and investors in mortgage servicing deserve the same standard everyone else takes for granted.

GLS was built on that premise. We built our core technology from scratch — on modern infrastructure, designed for the way lending actually works today. Not patched. Not retrofitted. Built new, because that was the only way to get it right.

Our philosophy
Technology should handle what it's good at.
Our People will be there to handle what it can't.
Automate the routine
Payment processing, reconciliation, compliance checks, document categorization, and draw workflows run automatically — so nothing falls through the cracks and nothing requires a manual workaround.
Hire for the human work
We hire people who are good at what technology can't replace — communication, judgment, and accountability when a borrower's situation doesn't fit a formula.
Better tools, better outcomes
When our team isn't fighting their own systems, they focus on the work that actually matters — and that's better for borrowers, better for investors, and better for the people doing the servicing.
